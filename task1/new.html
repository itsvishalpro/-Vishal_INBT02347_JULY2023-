Specification

These features can vary depending on the brand, model, and year of the television. Always check the specific product details to know exactly what features a particular TV offers.

  • Display Technology: LED, OLED, QLED, or MicroLED.
  • Resolution: HD (720p), Full HD (1080p), 4K (2160p), or 8K.
  • Screen Size: Diagonal measurement in inches or centimeters.
  • Refresh Rate: Typically 60Hz, 120Hz, or higher for smoother motion.
  • HDR (High Dynamic Range): Enhanced contrast and color range for more lifelike visuals.
  • Built-in Speakers: Audio quality, virtual surround sound, and Dolby support.
  • HDMI Ports: Number of inputs for connecting devices like gaming consoles, Blu-ray players, etc.
  • USB Ports: For connecting external drives, media playback, and device charging.
  • Good battery life
  • Voice Control: Integration with virtual assistants like Alexa, Google Assistant, or Bixby.
  • Operating System: Examples include Android TV, Tizen (Samsung), webOS (LG).
